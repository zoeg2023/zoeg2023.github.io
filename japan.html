JAPAN🍱

Japan is an archipelago in easetern Asia. The four main islands there are Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, and Kyusha.

Tokyo🌸

  • Tokyo is located in the center of the country in teh region of Kanto on the island of Honshu.
  • It is one of the most populated cities in the world. it is famous for its modern architecture and delicious Japanese food.

Niseko⛷

  • Niseko is a ski townn in Hokkaido. The area is most popularly visited for winter sports such as skiing and snowboarding.
  • Many people love the resort because it is open year-round to enjoy the snow!

Osaka🏯

  • Osaka is located in the south of Japan and about a six hour drive from Tokyo.
  • The city is home to various sweet, sour, savory, and more street foods. There are many street food markets that you can stop by.
  • One of the famous landmarks is the Osaka Castle whch is from the sixteenth century. The castle played a large role in the unification of Japan.
